Our Services

We offer premium ancient-style banquet services, wedding catering, custom private menus, and holiday celebration feasts. Winny's Catering merges traditional Chinese royal flavors with modern culinary artistry.

Cultural Inspiration

Our menu draws inspiration from the dining traditions of ancient Chinese beauties and Japanese floral art. We combine Tang and Song dynasty cuisine, Ming and Qing dynasty pastries, and Japanese wagashi to create a feast for both the eyes and the palate.
